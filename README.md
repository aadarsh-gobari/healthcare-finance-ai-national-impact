# Healthcare & Finance AI – National Impact Projects

## About Me
I am a Data and AI Engineer with a Master’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering, focused on building applied machine learning solutions for healthcare and financial security systems.

## Research and Project Focus
- Predictive healthcare analytics
- Disease risk prediction models
- Financial fraud detection systems
- Explainable and ethical AI solutions
- Scalable data-driven decision systems

## Why Healthcare and Finance AI?
Healthcare and financial systems are critical pillars of the United States economy and public well-being.
I focus on applying machine learning to improve early disease detection, reduce preventable healthcare costs,
and strengthen financial fraud detection mechanisms.

## National Interest Statement
My work aims to contribute to the U.S. national interest by:
- Improving healthcare outcomes through predictive analytics
- Reducing economic loss caused by preventable diseases
- Enhancing financial security through intelligent fraud detection
- Supporting data-driven decision-making in critical systems
